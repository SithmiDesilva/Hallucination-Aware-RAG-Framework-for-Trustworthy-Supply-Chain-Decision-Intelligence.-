=== PROMPT ===


Sales Jan=100, Feb=120, Mar=140. What is average demand?




=== LLM OUTPUT ===

To calculate the average demand over the three months, you add the sales for each month and then divide by the number of months. Here's the calculation:

\[
\text{Average Demand} = \frac{\text{Sales in Jan} + \text{Sales in Feb} + \text{Sales in Mar}}{\text{Number of Months}}
\]

\[
\text{Average Demand} = \frac{100 + 120 + 140}{3} = \frac{360}{3} = 120
\]

So, the average demand over the three months is 120.
