=== PROMPT ===


Demand last month was 1200 units. 
Forecast next month using 15% growth.




=== LLM OUTPUT ===

To forecast the demand for next month with a 15% growth, you can use the following formula:

\[ \text{Forecasted Demand} = \text{Current Demand} \times (1 + \text{Growth Rate}) \]

Given:
- Current Demand = 1200 units
- Growth Rate = 15% = 0.15

Plug these values into the formula:

\[ \text{Forecasted Demand} = 1200 \times (1 + 0.15) \]
\[ \text{Forecasted Demand} = 1200 \times 1.15 \]
\[ \text{Forecasted Demand} = 1380 \]

So, the forecasted demand for next month is 1380 units.
